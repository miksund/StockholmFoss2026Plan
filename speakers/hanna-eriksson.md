## About

Developer working with Swedish public transport APIs professionally, which means intimate knowledge of their quirks, limitations, and occasional excellence. The talk is an honest assessment: what's good, what's frustrating, and what needs improvement.

Advocates for better open data from Swedish transit authorities. Some agencies have excellent APIs; others make simple queries painful. Sharing specific examples (naming names) of good and bad practices.

Side project: building tools that make Swedish transit data more accessible for app developers.
