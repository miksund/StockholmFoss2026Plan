## About

Infrastructure architect at Region Stockholm, the regional authority responsible for healthcare in the Stockholm area. Specializing in automation for healthcare IT environments, where compliance, security, and reliability requirements are among the strictest in any industry.

Working at the intersection of modern DevOps practices and highly regulated healthcare systems. Bringing Ansible automation to environments where change management is measured in months, vendor relationships are complex, and the systems literally keep people alive.

Passionate about proving that healthcare IT doesn't have to be stuck in the past, while respecting the legitimate reasons for caution and compliance requirements.
