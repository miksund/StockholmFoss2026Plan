## About

A/V team lead for StockholmFoss, responsible for live streaming, recording, and ensuring all sessions are captured and shared with the global community. Using exclusively open source A/V tools wherever possible.

Background in video production, applying professional skills to make community conference recordings look professional on a volunteer budget. Building on lessons from other community conferences (FOSDEM, foss-north) that have solved A/V at scale.

Passionate about making conference content accessible: live streaming for those who can't attend, archived videos for future reference, and ensuring recordings are released quickly under open licenses.
