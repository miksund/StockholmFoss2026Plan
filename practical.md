## Getting There

<div class="info-grid">
  <div class="info-card">

  </div>
  <div class="info-card">

  </div>
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Bike</h3>
    <p>Bike parking is available outside all KTH buildings. Stockholm City Bikes (citybikes.se) has stations nearby.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>From Arlanda Airport</h3>

## Code of Conduct

<div class="coc-summary">
  <h3>Our Commitment</h3>
  <p>StockholmFoss is dedicated to providing a <strong>harassment-free conference experience for everyone</strong>, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or technology choices.</p>
  <p><strong>We do not tolerate harassment of conference participants in any form.</strong></p>
</div>

### Expected Behavior

- Be kind and respectful to all participants
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be mindful of your surroundings and fellow attendees
- Alert organizers if you witness or experience harassment

### Reporting

If you experience or witness harassment:

1. **In person:** Find an organizer (wearing a bright orange lanyard) or go to the registration desk
2. **By email:** coc@stockholmfoss.se
3. **Emergency:** Call 112 (Swedish emergency services)

Reports will be handled confidentially. We will take appropriate action to address any violations.

## Weather

Late May in Stockholm typically sees temperatures between 12-18°C (54-64°F) with long daylight hours (sunrise around 4:00, sunset around 21:30). Rain is possible, so bring a light jacket. The venue is fully indoors.

## Nearby Accommodation

Stockholm has many hotels and hostels. Areas convenient to KTH include:

- **Östermalm** - Walking distance, upscale options
- **City Center** - 10 minutes by T-bana
- **Södermalm** - 15 minutes by T-bana, many budget options

Book early as May is a popular time to visit Stockholm!
