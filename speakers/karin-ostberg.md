## About

Head of Open Source Strategy at DIGG (Myndigheten för digital förvaltning), the Swedish government agency responsible for digital government transformation. Leading Sweden's efforts to increase the use of open source in public sector IT and coordinate with other Nordic countries on shared digital solutions.

Background in both government IT and the private sector, bringing practical experience of what works and what doesn't when deploying open source in large organizations. Believes open source is essential for digital sovereignty, cost efficiency, and innovation in government services.

Focused on moving beyond policy documents to actual implementation: helping agencies adopt open source tools, share code across government, and contribute back to the projects they depend on.
