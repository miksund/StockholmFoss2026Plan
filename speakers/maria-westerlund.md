## About

Executive Director of Open Source Sweden, the industry association representing Swedish open source companies and advocating for open source adoption across the Swedish economy. Working at the intersection of technology, policy, and business to create a healthier ecosystem for open source in Sweden.

Previous career in software development and tech management, which grounds policy work in practical understanding of what open source companies actually need. Believes Sweden has unique strengths in open source (history, talent, values) that could be better leveraged with the right policy environment.

Currently focused on the EU Cyber Resilience Act and its implications for Swedish open source, advocating for regulations that protect consumers without crushing volunteer developers and small companies.
