## About

Open source maintainer with 15+ years of experience contributing to developer tools and infrastructure projects. Core maintainer of several Ruby gems and active contributor to the Go ecosystem. Passionate advocate for software freedom, maintainer sustainability, and keeping the human element central to open source development.

Frequent speaker at European FOSS conferences on topics including maintainer burnout, community governance, and the intersection of AI tools with open source workflows. Believes strongly that open source is fundamentally about people, not just code.

Currently focused on understanding how AI coding assistants are reshaping contribution patterns and what this means for the future of community-driven development.
