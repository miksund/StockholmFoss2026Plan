## About

Program committee member for StockholmFoss, bringing deep experience from the Qt and embedded open source communities. Author of "Foundations of Qt Development" and long-time speaker at conferences including Akademy, FOSDEM, and foss-north.

One of the founders of foss-north, Gothenburg's FOSS conference, now collaborating with StockholmFoss to strengthen the Swedish open source event landscape. Believes Sweden should have multiple strong FOSS gatherings.

Focus on the Development and Infrastructure tracks, leveraging connections in the embedded and desktop Linux communities to attract quality speakers.
