## About

Digital learning consultant at Skolverket (Swedish National Agency for Education), specializing in open source software deployment in Swedish schools. Leading the evaluation and support programs for municipalities that have adopted LibreOffice and other open source tools in educational settings.

With a background as a teacher and school IT coordinator, brings practical classroom experience to discussions about educational technology. Understands both the potential and the challenges of open source in schools: the pedagogical benefits, the training requirements, and the institutional resistance.

Focused on honest assessment of what works, what doesn't, and what needs to change for open source to succeed in education. Not an ideological advocate but a pragmatic evaluator of educational technology choices.
