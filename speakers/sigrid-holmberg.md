## About

Security architect at the Swedish Tax Agency (Skatteverket) specializing in software supply chain security and Software Bill of Materials (SBOM) implementation. Leading the agency's efforts to inventory, track, and secure the open source components used in critical government infrastructure.

With 12 years in information security and a background in software development, brings both technical depth and practical experience to the challenge of securing complex software ecosystems. Advocate for transparent, standards-based approaches to supply chain security that work for both government and open source communities.

Active contributor to international SBOM standardization efforts and frequent speaker on practical implementation of supply chain security in large organizations.
