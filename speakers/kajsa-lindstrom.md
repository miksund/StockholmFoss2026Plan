## About

Database engineer turned technology historian with unique first-hand experience from MySQL AB's formative years. Spent a decade building database systems before transitioning to documenting and preserving the history of Swedish technology innovation.

Currently writing a book on the Nordic open source movement, tracing the cultural and technical factors that led Sweden to become a hub for database technology, telecommunications software, and developer tools. Believes understanding our technical history is essential for making better decisions about our digital future.

Combines deep technical expertise with historical research methods, conducting oral histories with pioneers of Swedish tech and archiving important documents and code from the early internet era.
