## About

Beekeeper and hobby hardware hacker who built open source beehive monitoring systems to track hive weight, temperature, humidity, and activity. Combining traditional beekeeping with IoT sensors and data analysis.

Project started from frustration with expensive proprietary monitoring systems. Built custom hardware with ESP32, load cells, and temperature sensors, publishing all designs and code open source.

Now part of small community of open source beekeeping technologists, sharing data and improvements across Europe.
